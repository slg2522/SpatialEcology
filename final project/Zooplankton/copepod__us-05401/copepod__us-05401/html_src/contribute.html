COPEPOD is seeking plankton abundance, biomass, and/or composition data (in electronic or paper form) and references leading to plankton data (e.g., "Smith et al. 1958 has numerous tables of zooplankton biomass").

Contributing Data is EASY!   No reformatting is necessary!
We accept incoming data in YOUR format, spreadsheet, log-book, whatever!

COPEPOD Contributors will receive:
  • early notification of COPEPOD data and product releases;
  • complimentary copies of future COPEPOD CD-ROM and Atlas products;
  • full credit for their contribution by inclusion in the Hall of Fame.
